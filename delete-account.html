Delete Account - JobHook

Last Updated: 05/01/2026

JobHook allows users to delete their account and request deletion of associated personal data.

1. Delete Your Account Inside the App

Users can delete their account directly inside the JobHook mobile app.

To delete your account, open the JobHook app and go to:

  • Profile
  • Settings
  • Delete Account

2. Request Account Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by contacting us by email.

Email: support@jobhook.com

Please include the email address used for your JobHook account so we can locate and process your request.

3. Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, JobHook may delete account-related data including:

  • Your account profile information
  • Your saved jobs
  • Your job application records
  • Uploaded profile or resume information, if applicable

4. Deletion Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

Some information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes.

5. Contact

For questions about account deletion or privacy, contact:

support@jobhook.com
