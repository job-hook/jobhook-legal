JobHook Job Posting Guidelines

Last updated: February 2026

By posting a job on JobHook, you agree to follow these guidelines. These rules help protect employers, job seekers, and the JobHook platform.

1. Genuine Job Opportunities Only

All job listings must represent real and legitimate employment opportunities. Fake jobs, misleading offers, or scam-related postings are strictly prohibited.

2. No Upfront Payments

Employers must not request application fees, registration fees, training fees, or any form of payment from job seekers before employment.

3. Accurate Job Information

  • Job title
  • Responsibilities
  • Location
  • Salary information (if provided)
  • Required experience or qualifications

Misleading or false information may result in removal of the job listing.

4. Non-Discrimination Policy

Job postings must not discriminate based on race, nationality, gender, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic.

5. Prohibited Content

The following are not allowed:

  • Illegal services or activities
  • Adult or explicit content
  • Weapons, drugs, or harmful services
  • Multi-level marketing schemes without clear disclosure

6. One Job Per Listing

Each job post must represent a single position. Multiple unrelated roles should be posted separately.

7. Review and Moderation

All job postings may be reviewed by JobHook administrators. JobHook reserves the right to edit, reject, or remove listings that violate these guidelines.

8. Employer Responsibility

By submitting a job, you confirm that:

  • You are authorized to hire for the position.
  • The information provided is accurate.
  • You agree to comply with JobHook policies.

9. Reporting Issues

If you encounter suspicious or inappropriate job postings, please contact JobHook support, https://job-hook.github.io/jobhook-legal/support.html
