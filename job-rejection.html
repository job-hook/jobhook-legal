Common reasons we reject job posts

  • Missing details: salary, location, job type, or requirements not clear.
  • Misleading information: unclear pay, unrealistic promises, or false claims.
  • Spam or duplicate posts: repeated postings with the same content.
  • Contact info in the job description: phone numbers/emails in the post (use the app messaging flow instead).
  • Discrimination or restricted content: content that violates policies or local laws.
  • Low quality formatting: all caps, symbols, or unreadable text.

How to fix and resubmit

  • Edit the job post and add missing fields.
  • Make salary and responsibilities clear.
  • Remove personal contact info from the description.
  • Submit again for review.
